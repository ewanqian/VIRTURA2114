VIRTURA 2114 is an **open-source digital worldbuilding project** that combines:

- **Quantum Temporal Layers** - Every location exists in three states: Blueprint (2025), Golden Age (2049), and Wasteland (2050)
- **Faction Politics** - Five digital factions vie for control, each with distinct ideologies

- **Complete Timeline** - 80+ events spanning 2021-2050
