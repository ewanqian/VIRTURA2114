VIRTURA 2114 Digital Spaceport Archive

An open-source cyberpunk worldbuilding project set in post-collapse 2050. Explore a fragmented digital civilization through 75+ documents, 80+ timeline events, and AI-powered interactive gameplay. Every location exists in three temporal states: Blueprint (2025), Golden Age (2049), and Wasteland (2050).

开源赛博朋克世界构建项目，设定于 2050 年大崩溃后。通过 75+ 个文档、80+ 个时间线事件和 AI 驱动的互动玩法，探索破碎的数字文明。每个地点存在三种时空状态：蓝图期 (2025)、黄金期 (2049)、废土期 (2050)。
